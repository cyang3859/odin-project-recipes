Creamy Tuscan White Bean Skillet

Home Page

Description

This creamy Tuscan white bean skillet is a quick and easy one-pan meal that's packed with flavor. It's made with white beans, spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, and a creamy garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) white beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the white beans, spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, vegetable broth, and heavy cream. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes, until heated through.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot and enjoy!
