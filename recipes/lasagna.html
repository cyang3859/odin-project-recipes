Description

This spinach artichoke lasagna is a delicious twist on the classic Italian dish. It's made with layers of fresh spinach, creamy ricotta cheese, and tender artichoke hearts, all nestled between sheets of lasagna noodles and topped with a rich marinara sauce.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Spray a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the chopped spinach, artichoke hearts, ricotta cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  5. Spread one-third of the spinach and artichoke mixture over the noodles. Top with one-third of the marinara sauce and one-third of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a layer of marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese on top. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
