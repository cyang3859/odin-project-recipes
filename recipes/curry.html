Vegan Jamaican Curry

Home Page

Description

This vegan Jamaican curry is a flavorful and hearty dish made with a variety of vegetables, spices, and coconut milk. It's perfect served over rice or with warm naan bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 can (14 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp allspice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the coconut oil over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the carrots, bell pepper, and zucchini, and cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.
  3. Stir in the chickpeas, coconut milk, curry powder, allspice, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Reduce heat to low and let the curry cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve hot over rice or with naan bread. Enjoy!
